Recipe(Bacon Grilled Sandwich)

Ingredients

Meat

  • Bacon
  • Chicken
  • Sausages

Veggies

  • Letuce
  • Tomato
  • Bread

Dairy

  • Mayo
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Combine hot mayo ingredients in a small bowl and whisk to combine. Set aside.
  2. Slice tomato into slices with a small knife. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and let rest for 5 minutes to let the excess moisture be released. Once released dab excess off with paper towel.
  3. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Sprinkle buttered side with red pepper flakes, onion powder, and garlic powder. Press in slightly so the spices will stick to the butter.
  4. Flip bread over and begin to prepare each sandwich. Spread on half with hot mayo sauce and place cheddar cheese on the other half.
  5. Add Monterey Jack cheese over the hot mayo then top with cooked bacon.
  6. Finally, sprinkle on fresh basil and then add sliced tomato. Close the two halves of the sandwich and repeat for the second sandwich.
  7. Once sandwiches are prepped, heat a medium sized skillet and add butter or nonstick cooking spray. Cook the sandwiches on low heat for 10 minutes, flipping halfway.
  8. Once the cheese has melted, turn the heat up for the last 5 minutes to get the outside of the bread nice and crispy.
